PBL PROJECT 2022-23 A11

This PBL project is developed by the students of PICT (FE11 A11 batch) with the aim of making investing and trading much easier. The project primarily targets young investors who may lack the means to start trading in the market with real money. To address this, we have developed a paper trading platform that allows users to gain experience without using real funds. The project's main feature is a bot capable of automated trading, which can help users grow their investments while they focus on other tasks. Through this initiative, the students hope to empower young individuals to develop valuable skills and a sound understanding of financial markets, paving the way for a more secure financial future.
